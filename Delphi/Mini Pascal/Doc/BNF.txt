Mini Pascal -> <assignment> | <if> | <for> | <do-while> | <while-do> | <function call>
<assignment> -> <identifier> := <expression>

<expression> --> <term> | <sign><term> | <term> <addoperator> <term>
<term> --> <factor> | <factor> <multoperator> <factor>
<factor> --> <number> | <id> | <function> |(<expression>)
<multoperator> --> * | / | %
<addoperator> --> + | -
